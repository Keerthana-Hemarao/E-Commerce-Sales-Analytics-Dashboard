# E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of e-commerce sales performance using an interactive dashboard. The goal is to provide clear business insights into sales trends, profitability, customer segments, product performance, and regional distribution to support strategic decision-making.

---

## Business Objectives
- Track year-to-date sales, profit, quantity, and profit margins  
- Identify top- and bottom-performing products  
- Analyze sales performance across regions and customer segments  
- Evaluate shipping and fulfillment effectiveness  
- Support data-driven sales and inventory optimization  

---

## Dashboard Pages
### 1. Sales Overview
- YTD Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Profit Margin  
- Year-over-Year sales trend  
- Category-level performance comparison  

### 2. Product Performance
- Top 5 products by sales  
- Bottom 5 products by sales  
- Product contribution to overall revenue  

### 3. Regional & Shipping Analysis
- Sales distribution by region and state  
- Sales by shipping type  
- Delivery performance impact on revenue  

---

## Key Business Insights
- Office Supplies is the highest revenue-generating category  
- Profit growth outperforms sales growth, indicating improved efficiency  
- Certain products consistently underperform and impact margins  
- West and East regions drive the majority of sales  
- Standard shipping accounts for the largest share of revenue  

---

## Business Impact
This dashboard enables stakeholders to:
- Quickly assess business performance  
- Identify revenue leakages and growth opportunities  
- Improve product and inventory decisions  
- Optimize regional and logistics strategies  

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI for dashboard development  
- Excel for data preparation and validation
